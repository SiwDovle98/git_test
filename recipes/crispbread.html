Crisp Bread

Homemade crisp bread which are thin, cripsy, will fuel you for a long time and taste delicious

  • Ground whole grain rye - 3dl
  • Rye flake (Can be changed with spelled flake or oatmeal) - 1 dl
  • Oatmeal - 2 dl
  • Sunflower seeds - 2 dl
  • Pumpkin seeds - 2 dl
  • Crused lin seeds - 1 dl
  • Sesame seeds - 1 dl
  • Sea salt - 1 tsp
  • Water - 7 1/2 dl
  1. Heat the oven to 160 degres hot air
  2. Measure all the dry ingredients and mix well, then add water
  3. Spread the batter on three baking trays lined with baking paper
  4. Feel free to sprinkle a little extra with seeds/kernels, and fry the shortbread at 160 degrees.
  5. After 10 min cut them into 4*5 pices and put back in
  6. Every 15 to 20 min rotate the trays
  7. The crackers are ready after approx. 60-70 minutes total cooking time, but this varies, so here you have to follow along towards the end of the cooking time.
