Sweet chilli sauce

Make your own sweet chilli sauce to cook with. This Thai condiment often contains fish sauce but weâ€™ve kept our version veggie

  • Red chilli - 3 stk
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Cloves of garlic - 2
  • Oil - 1 tbs
  • Pinapple - 3 dl
  • Vinegar - 1/2 dl
  • Sugar - 3 dl
  • Dried chilli flakes - 4 tbs
  • Cornstarch - 2 tsb
  • Cold water - 4 tsb
  1. Finely chop the chilli and mash in a mortar with salt and garlic to a coarse mass. Fry quickly in a pan with oil while stirring, approx. 30 seconds.
  2. Add pineapple juice, vinegar and sugar and bring to a boil. Stir in chili flakes and simmer for approx. 10 minutes. Make sure it doesn't boil for too long, then you'll get a crack rather than a sauce.
  3. Stir Maizena into cold water and season the sauce with the mixture, stirring constantly. Continue cooking until the sauce is thick.
  4. Pour the sauce into tight glasses and cool. The sauce can be kept in the fridge for several months.
