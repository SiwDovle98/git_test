Crisp Bread

Coconut date balls are little balls of nutrition made with dates and nuts and then coated in shredded coconut - an easy-to-make sweet & healthy vegan snack!

  • Fresh dates - 200g
  • Peanuts - 100g
  • Cocoa - 3-4 tsp
  • Maldon salt - 1/2 tsp
  1. Put all the ingredients in a food processor or powerful blender and process to a smooth and sticky dough.
  2. Roll the dough out into a sausage, cut into pieces and roll into mouthful balls between your hands (preferably slightly damp hands).
  3. The balls are good as they are, but you can roll them in cocoa powder, coconut, chopped nuts or similar of course you want
